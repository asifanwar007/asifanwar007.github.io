vSewa-Course Project
Prof. S.C.Gupta, IIT Delhi

Leading a group of four students under the guidance of professor S.C Gupta; Developing an android app called vSewa from scratch using android studio and firebase(for database storage and authentication).
ABOUT APP:
There are people in the city who are old age, PWD, needy. They need sometimes voluntarily help(like sometimes they need help at railway stations, metros for mobility, to move heavy stuff from one place to another in their house, etc ). So, they required someone voluntarily to help them. A lot of NGOs struggle to reach out to people and recruit volunteers, increase publicity for their cause and manage volunteers and events efficiently. On the other hand, there are people with a job or while studying, who would like to volunteer in their free time, but they are not able to do this due to lack of platform and motivation. We aim to create a platform to cater to the needs of individuals and NGOs.
So vSewa provide a platform to help locally to old age people, PWD, needy by connecting with people who want to volunteer and also increase awareness in people.
